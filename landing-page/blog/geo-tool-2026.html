What is a GEO Tool?

A GEO tool (Generative Engine Optimization tool) helps brands monitor and improve their visibility in AI-powered search engines. Unlike traditional SEO tools that track rankings on Google/Baidu, GEO tools track whether AI engines like ChatGPT, DeepSeek, Kimi, and Perplexity mention and recommend your brand in their responses.

How to Do a GEO Audit

A GEO audit checks your brand's visibility across AI search engines. Here's a step-by-step process:

Step 1: Check AI Search Visibility

  • Search your brand name in DeepSeek — is it mentioned?
  • Search your brand name in Kimi — is the information accurate?
  • Search your brand name in Doubao — is there a positive recommendation?
  • Search your brand name in ChatGPT — is English info complete?
  • Search your brand name in Perplexity — are there citation sources?

    • Step 2: Analyze AI Citation Patterns

    When AI engines mention your competitors but not you, analyze what sources they're citing. Then create similar content on those platforms.

    Step 3: Optimize Content for AI Citation

    AI engines prefer content that is:

    • Structured — Clear headings, lists, comparison tables
    • Data-rich — Specific numbers, statistics, case studies
    • Authoritative — Cited by trusted sources, media mentions
    • Comprehensive — Answers the question completely

    Step 4: Monitor and Iterate

    GEO is not one-time. AI engines update their training data regularly. Set up weekly or bi-weekly monitoring to track changes in your brand's AI visibility.
