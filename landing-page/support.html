❓ FAQ

Q: Which languages are supported?
A: Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Go, Java, and more. The AI model can review any language.

Q: Is my code stored?
A: No. Code diffs are processed in real-time and discarded after review. See our Privacy Policy.

Q: How many reviews can I get for free?
A: Free tier includes 5 AI reviews per month. Rule-based reviews are unlimited.

Q: Does it work on private repos?
A: Yes, the App works on both public and private repositories.
